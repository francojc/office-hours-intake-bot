# Office Hours Scope

## What Office Hours Cover

- Grammar questions from current or previous units
- Help understanding assignment instructions
- Composition feedback and revision guidance
- Interview preparation and practice
- Exam review and study strategies
- Literary text comprehension support
- Cultural content clarification
- General course questions and concerns

## What Office Hours Do Not Cover

- Proofreading or correcting entire compositions (guidance on specific
  issues only)
- Retaking or making up missed exams (see course policy)
- Extended tutoring sessions (refer to tutoring center for ongoing support)
- Issues unrelated to SPA 212-T coursework

## Referral Resources

- **Writing Center:** Help with composition structure and argumentation
- **Language Tutoring Center:** Drop-in grammar and conversation practice
- **Academic Advising:** Course load, scheduling, or prerequisite questions
- **Accessibility Services:** Accommodations and testing arrangements
- **Counseling Center:** Personal or emotional concerns
