# SPA 212-T: Grammar and Skill Topics

Course: SPA 212 — Exploring the Hispanic World
Textbook: Punto y Aparte (6th ed., McGraw Hill, 2020)
Supplementary: Lecturas Literarias (McGraw Hill, 2006)

## Unit 1: Perspectivas (Chapters 1-2, Weeks 1-6)

### Descripciones (Descriptions)

- Adjective-noun agreement (gender, number)
- Ser vs. estar (identity/characteristics vs. state/location)
- Past participles used as adjectives
- Uses and omissions of definite/indefinite articles

### Comparaciones (Comparisons)

- Comparative structures (más/menos... que, tan... como)
- Superlatives
- Irregular comparatives (mejor, peor, mayor, menor)

### Narración básica (Basic Narration)

- Preterite formation (regular and irregular)
- Imperfect formation (regular and irregular)
- Preterite vs. imperfect: choosing the correct aspect
- Verbs with meaning changes in preterite vs. imperfect (conocer, saber, poder, querer)

### Narración compleja (Complex Narration)

- Present perfect and pluperfect tenses
- Hace... que constructions for expressing time duration

### Vocabulary Themes

- Physical and personality descriptions
- Family relationships (relaciones familiares)

### Literature

- "La colmena"
- "Con los ojos cerrados"

### Cultural Regions

- Spain (Spanish Civil War, Salvador Dalí)
- Caribbean (Cuba, Dominican Republic, Venezuela)

## Unit 2: Pasiones y sentimientos (Chapters 3-4, Weeks 10-15)

### Reacciones (Reactions)

- Present subjunctive formation (regular and irregular)
- Past subjunctive formation
- Subjunctive in noun clauses:
  - Expressions of influence (querer que, recomendar que)
  - Expressions of emotion (alegrarse de que, tener miedo de que)
  - Expressions of doubt (dudar que, no creer que)

### Recomendaciones y mandatos (Recommendations and Commands)

- Formal commands (Ud./Uds.)
- Informal commands (tú, affirmative and negative)
- Subjunctive for recommendations

### Los gustos (Preferences and Tastes)

- Direct object pronouns
- The personal "a"
- Indirect object pronouns
- Double object pronouns
- Gustar and similar verbs (encantar, molestar, importar, fascinar)

### Las opiniones (Opinions)

- Subjunctive in adjective clauses
- Subjunctive vs. indicative in opinion expressions

### Vocabulary Themes

- Sentimental/romantic relationships (relaciones sentimentales)
- Obligations and free time (obligaciones y tiempo libre)

### Literature

- "Cristina Martínez"
- "La fiaca"

### Cultural Regions

- Mexico (Mexican Revolution, Frida Kahlo, Diego Rivera)
- Southern Cone (Chile, Argentina)

---

## Unit 3: El mundo actual y el porvenir (Chapters 5-6, Weeks 21-26)

### Hacer hipótesis (Making Hypotheses)

- Conditional tense formation and usage
- Past subjunctive with sequence of tenses
- Si clauses (if-then constructions)
- Adverbial clauses

### El futuro (Future Discourse)

- Future tense formation and usage
- Expressing future plans and predictions

### Posibilidades y responsabilidades (Possibilities and Responsibilities)

- Integration of all communicative functions: description, comparison,
  narration, reaction, opinion, and hypotheses
- Subjunctive in adverbial clauses (para que, antes de que, etc.)

### Vocabulary Themes

- Problems and solutions
- Careers and the workplace (carreras, mundo laboral)
- The future, possibilities, responsibilities

### Literature

- "El banquete"
- "Única mirando al mar"

### Cultural Regions

- Andean region (Peru, Bolivia)
- Central America

## Assessment Types

### Exams (3 total, 45% of grade)

Each exam has two parts administered on separate days:
- Grammar/vocabulary exam
- Culture/literature exam

Schedule:
- Exam 1: Chapters 1-2 (Weeks 7-9)
- Exam 2: Chapters 3-4 (Weeks 18-20)
- Exam 3: Final exam (Week 30)

### Written Compositions (2 total, 25% of grade)

- Escritura I: assigned Week 16
- Escritura II: assigned Week 27

### Individual Interviews (2 total, 10% of grade)

- Entrevista I: Week 16-17
- Entrevista II: Week 27-28

### Oral Presentation (1 total, 5% of grade)

- Student-led cultural presentations throughout the semester

### Participation (15% of grade)

- Daily in-class engagement and preparation
